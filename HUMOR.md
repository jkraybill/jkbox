## JKLES Scale

**11/10** - Perfection. Klein bottle territory. Recursive meta-comedy singularity.
**10/10** - Excellent. JK actually laughed out loud.
**9/10** - Great. Strong amusement, would share with friends.
**8/10** - Good. Solid chuckle, amused smirk.
**7/10** - Acceptable. Mildly amusing, smile-worthy.
**6/10** - Passable. Recognized as humor, but didn't fully land.
**5/10** - Neutral. "That was a joke?" territory.
**4/10** - Weak. Groan-inducing, maybe too forced.
**3/10** - Bad. Fell flat, awkward silence.
**2/10** - Cringe. Actively unfunny.
**1/10** - Disaster. Comedy graveyard. Never again.

**Aim for 9-11/10. Anything below 7/10 warrants calibration.**
